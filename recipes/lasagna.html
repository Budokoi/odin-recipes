Lasagna

Lasagna is a popular dish among the world orginating from italy

Eaten by many around the world but this is how I make my lasagna

I first start out with making the sauce

Lasagna sauce

  • 1 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 medium red onions, finely diced
  • 2 large cloves minced garlic
  • 8ounces pancetta, diced
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1½ cups good red wine, preferably Italian
  • 2 28-ounce cans Italian plum tomatoes
  • 3 tablespoons tomato paste
  • ¾ pound ground sirloin
  • ¼ cup freshly grated pecorino Romano
  • 2 eggs
  • 10 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only, washed and dried
  • 2 large whole cloves garlic
  • ½ cup flour
  • 1 pound Italian sausage, a mix of hot and sweet

I then layer it between lasagna and put it in the oven
